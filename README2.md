# 🚀 AI Decision Systems Portfolio

---

## 🧠 Overview

This repository showcases **production-grade AI and decision systems** designed to convert real-time data into **actionable decisions and execution** under real-world constraints.

These are not experiments.

They are **live system architectures** built to operate under:

- latency constraints  
- capital constraints  
- risk constraints  
Data → Features → Decision → Risk → Execution → Monitoring


The focus is on **systems that make decisions and act on them**, not just models that generate predictions.

---

## 🎯 Portfolio Structure

This portfolio is intentionally divided into:

### 🔴 Capital Decision Systems (Core)

Systems whose purpose is to **generate capital growth**

### 🔵 Supporting Infrastructure

Systems that enable **data collection, execution, and system reliability**

# 🔴 Capital Decision Systems

---

## 1. Easy ORB 0DTE — High-Frequency Capital Allocation System

**Primary production system for capital growth**

A cloud-deployed execution system that:

- captures opening range breakout (ORB) structures  
- evaluates tradeability in real-time  
- executes ETF + 0DTE options trades  

**Key capabilities:**

- 36,786 lines of production code  
- dual-strategy system (ETF + options overlay)  
- 14+ automated exit conditions  
- real-time options pricing (30s updates)  
- ADV-based position sizing + risk gating  

**Performance:**

- +73.69% weekly validated return  
- 91% winning day consistency  
- 96% drawdown reduction  

**Core insight:**

This system demonstrates **decision-making under extreme constraints**:

- minutes-level timing  
- capital exposure  
- volatility uncertainty  

It is a **real execution system**, not a backtest.

## 2. Easy Kalshibot — Prediction Market Decision System

A probabilistic decision system for **event-driven markets**.

**Core concepts:**

- probability vs market pricing (edge detection)  
- Bayesian updates from new information  
- Kelly-based capital allocation  

**What it demonstrates:**

- decision-making under uncertainty  
- market-based probability reasoning  
- constrained capital deployment  

**Key insight:**

Markets are treated as **probability surfaces**, not price charts.

## Core Principles

- **Decisions > predictions**  
  Systems must act, not just infer  

- **Constraints define reality**  
  Latency, capital, and risk shape outcomes  

- **Deterministic execution**  
  Idempotent, verifiable system behavior  

- **Separation of concerns**  
  Strategy, data, risk, and execution are independent  

- **Fail-safe systems**  
  Protect capital before optimizing performance
